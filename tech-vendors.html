Why Technology?

Technology is LAUSD's largest contract category by value. While some technology spending is necessary, several contracts have raised serious concerns — including the AllHere AI chatbot (FBI investigation), the Yondr phone pouch program (widely criticized), and PowerSchool (largest student data breach in US history). Meanwhile, the district claims it cannot afford fair teacher pay.
