Contract Database

Fact-checking in progress.

We’re fact-checking the contract data.

This database was built using AI-assisted extraction from official LAUSD board documents. Community reviewers have identified errors in some contract entries — including amounts incorrectly attributed to the wrong agenda items. We are verifying every contract against its source document before making the database searchable again.

Every contract on this site links to its original board report. Our goal is accuracy, not speed. We’d rather take the database offline temporarily than publish information that hasn’t been verified.
